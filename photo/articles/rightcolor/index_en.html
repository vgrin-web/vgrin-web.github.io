"Right" color

In each pair one frame was taken with the "right" camera (S5Pro, Foveon and the like...) and processed in the converter with the default settings, except for the white balance.
That is it was not exposed to any interference from the photographer and was not "spoiled" in any way - it is a pure result of the machine.
How the second frame was processed will be described later.
Shooting conditions; ISO 100 to 200, same lens (except for camera with Foveon sensor), same aperture.
You are asked to determine which frame was taken with the "right" camera and which was not.
